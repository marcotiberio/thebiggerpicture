Since the 2000s, track and field athletes at major competitions wear their names as well as their numbers on their bibs. Powerlifters wear a bib benchpress shirt across their chest area to help them lift more weight. In Netball, bibs are used by the umpire to identify players' positions so it can be determined who is within their allowed area.

The Bigger Picture is the photography blog where you will enjoy big photos, like you rarely find on the Web, and also learn more about their contexts by zooming in on selected details of the photos and read curious information, facts, anecdotes about them.
“We never approach an image with a blank mind but with a mind already primed with memories, knowledge, prejudices. […] The frame of the photograph encloses a space, a world, which we can enter (in our imaginations)”. […] And “if display context can influence the meaning of a photograph, the photograph can also influence the meaning of the context”.
The Bigger Picture wants to play with this relationship and let you explore, dig into and engage with the picture.
Curated by Mauro Bedoni, website developed by Marco Tiberio/Defrost Studio.
